How to import your playlists into Spotify

Once you've downloaded a CSV from Strata, you can use Soundiiz to create that playlist directly in your Spotify account. It's free and takes about a minute.

01
Download your CSV from Strata
Open any playlist or run a search, then click the ↓ Download CSV button. Save the file somewhere easy to find.
02
Go to Soundiiz and sign in with Spotify
Head to soundiiz.com and click Start Now. Sign in using your Spotify account — Soundiiz only needs permission to create playlists, it won't modify anything else.
03
Click "Import Playlist" in the upper right
Once you're logged in, look for the Import Playlist button in the upper right corner of the Soundiiz dashboard.
04
Select "From File"
In the import options, choose From File. Then select the CSV file you downloaded from Strata.
05
Follow the prompts and export to Spotify
Soundiiz will read your file and match the tracks. Review the results, give your playlist a name, and choose Spotify as the destination. That's it — your playlist will appear in Spotify within seconds.
Good to know

The CSV from Strata includes Spotify URIs — the exact unique ID for each song. This means Soundiiz can match tracks with complete accuracy, no guessing from song titles. You won't get wrong versions or missing tracks.
